About Us

In addition to the company owner’s 10 years experience in Home Textile Sourcing business, Anka Consultancy&Textile were founded in 1997. Since then we have been working with the best suppliers and merchandisers to accomodate our customers’ needs.

Anka Consultancy Textiles have been in Textile Sourcing business since 1997. Our company are deeply working with and focusing on the suppliers who can work with honesty, competency and quality.

 We have a complete team of trained Merchandisers and Quality Controllers to look after your orders at every stage of the process. Currently, we are working as sourcing agent for reputed importers based Europe, USA, and Australia.
For the home textile we offer everything from yarn to made-ups including designs, product development, documentation and logisctic.
