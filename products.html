Products

We are in all levels of Home Textiles including row materials (yarn and grey fabrics) to made-ups. It will be better to study our product range activity in 4 main categories. 

YARN

Different kind of compositions mainly 100% Cotton but also Polycotton (major percentage is Polyster) and CVC (major percentage is Cotton) are our principal compositions next to different blends.

Our yarn types are used at both weaving and knitting fields.

About yarn numbers again we work in very wide interval as from NE 6 up to NE 100.

Our sourcing countries are variable depend on composition, yarn number and where to use.

Our selling is to all over the World industrialists means weavers and traders. 

GREY FABRICS

All kind of grey fabrics produced from all kind of different yarns; means different compositions, different yarn numbers, different constructions.

Narrow Widths (90cm) to very wider widths (340 cm) weaving is under our possibilities.

We had both optional weaving units possibilities like Sulzer and Airjet.

Packaging can be rolls or pallets; depend on our customer needs. 

PROCESSED FABRICS

Being next step of grey fabrics, we can serve all kind of processing to our all kind of grey fabrics.

As a processing; bleaching-dyeing-printing are our capabilities under their sub categorization like;
Bleaching; simple or optical white Dyeing; Pigment, Reactive, Indanthrene (VAT)

Printing; Pigment, Reactive, Indanthrene(VAT)

Finally two principal procedures of processing are piece dyed processing and yarn dyed processing. 

MADE-UPS

All kind of Made-Ups as Bed Linen; Quilt Covers, Flat Sheets, Fitted Sheets, Pillow Cases
Bath Linen; Towels, Bathrobes, Bathmaths
Tables Linen; Table Clothes, Napkins, Runners
Bed Covers; Bedspreads, Quilts
Upholstery&Curtains;

All kind of compositions as 100% Cotton, Polycotton, CVC and other compositions

All kind of range as Standart, Medium to High-end range
